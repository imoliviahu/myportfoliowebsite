Electric Zoo Rebrand

A trendy and lively spin on the brand identity of New York's electronic music festival, Electric Zoo. For my most recent branding project, I rebranded Electric Zoo Festival, which is a popular electric dance festival in New York City. I felt the original branding lacked the energy and trendiness of the young generation so I wanted to bring that into my concept. Raves are an integral part of the younger generation as well as the Asian American community. It is a place where everyone bonds over music, connects with friends, and releases their stress from everyday life. I incorporated that feeling into my typography with two contrasting typefaces, one that is slanted to show speed and excitement, and the other explosive typeface to show an outburst of energy. In terms of color choice, I went with complimentary bright colors that resemble the lasers and neon lights you see at night at the festival to evoke a sense of nostalgia and essence of a rave. Finally, with the animation, I wanted to keep it simple but eyecatching and trendy.
