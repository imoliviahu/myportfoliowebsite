BOOK DESIGN
"The Milky Way"
(2021)
Photography and physical scans of printed book. Designed with Adobe InDesign and Illustrator. Text and images from Wikipedia article, "The Milky Way"
Space has always fascinated me by its beauty and mysteriousness. Through the design, composition, and typography of this book, I wanted to emphasize the vastness of space, calculations, and futuristic qualities that are tied to the study of the universe.
PHONE CASE DESIGN
"Social Media is Fake"
(2021)
My design follows the mantra, "Social Media is Fake," which is an important message to spread during this day and age. Communicated through a phone case, I want people to always have a reminder that they are seen and understood.
Growing up with social media, it has created tolls on my mental health. I constantly find myself comparing my life to a versions of others that they crafted on their social media profile. With this thought in mind, I wanted to express how social media is like a broken mirror where it does not reflect the real side of people's lives, but a fake persona.
